About Us

Goldblat Diamonds is a globally renowned leader in high-end diamonds and jewelry, specializing in natural fancy-colored diamonds and gemstones. Founded in 1979, this close-knit family business—led by Leibish, his wife Rosi, and their children—has become a trusted name in the industry. Today, Goldblat Diamonds proudly serves as a premier supplier to esteemed jewelers, designers, and manufacturers worldwide, combining decades of expertise with a personal touch.

Goldblat Diamonds offers one of the most extensive online collections of colored diamonds, featuring an array of exquisite fancy and ideal cuts in a spectrum of hues, clarities, and sizes to suit a range of budgets. Specializing in rare Argyle Pink and Canary Yellow diamonds, Goldblat Diamonds sources its stones from trusted mines in Australia, Africa, India, and Russia. Every diamond is ethically sourced, adhering to UN Resolutions and ensuring compliance with international conflict-free standards.

Example Diamonds:

  • 8.88 carat, Fancy Intense Yellow Diamond, Cushion Shape, VS1 Clarity, GIA
  • Fancy Yellow, Radiant Pave Cross-Over Diamond Dress Ring (3.92Ct TW)

At the turn of the 21st century, Goldblat Diamonds expanded into jewelry manufacturing, designing and creating exquisite pieces featuring color diamonds, each expertly graded for size and color. While their color diamond engagement rings are customer favorites, the company offers a diverse range of jewelry, from everyday elegance to haute couture masterpieces.

Goldblat Diamonds is a regular participant in prestigious trade shows and the exclusive Argyle Pink Diamond Tender, securing some of the world’s rarest gems. Highlights include:

  • A matching pair of Argyle pink diamonds over a carat each in 2017.
  • The first internally flawless Argyle diamond in over 20 years in 2016.
  • A total of 67 Argyle Tender diamonds acquired between 2009 and 2017.

With offices strategically located worldwide—including the Israel Diamond Exchange, New York City, Sydney Australia, and Central Hong Kong—Goldblat Diamonds ensures a global presence. The company also collaborates with trusted partners in Belgium and Mumbai, strengthening its international reach.
